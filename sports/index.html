The 4x100 Relay


Rules

  1. Sprinter must stay in lane
  2. Baton must reach the finish line in order to be counted
  3. Sprinter 1 (S1) must put baton in opposite handling hand of Sprinter 2 (S2) and so on

How to play

A sprinter is situated every 100 meters on a 400 meter track. They pass a baton to each other while sprinting around the track.

Soccer


Rules

Rules of soccer:

  1. Regulation size soccer ball (Size 5) must be used for U13 and up
  2. Maximum number of players on field: 11; Minimum: 7
  3. Players must wear same uniform
  4. Soccer cleats are required; Same with shin-guards
  5. Ref has to enforce laws
  6. Linesmen may assist referees
  7. Duration of game is 90 minutes
  8. A kick-off starts the game
  9. Kick-off players must be on their side of the field
