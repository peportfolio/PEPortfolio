The 4x100 Relay


Rules

1. Sprinter must stay in lane
2. Baton must reach the finish line in order to be counted
3. Sprinter 1 (S1) must put baton in opposite handling hand of Sprinter 2 (S2) and so on

How to play

A sprinter is situated every 100 meters on a 400 meter track. They pass a baton to each other while sprinting around the track.
